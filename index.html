DRC Investment Summit
2022 Edition

The DRC Investment Summit will bring over 1250 high level participants targeting over US$10 Billion worth of investment. The Summit will feature private and PPP infrastructure development projects in the Investor Exchange Sessions - DRC is open for business and it is moving towards business friendly policies and engagement.

From 27 - 30 June 2022

@ Kinshasa

Main Program

Pipeline Development

Pooling the collective efforts of development agencies, government departments, development institutions and advisory firms to source projects and deal screening

Project Bankability

Using our expert and in-depth due diligence to ensure the bankability of private and PPP projects

Project Preparation

Scaling up technical assistance, process harmonisation, funding and grants for feasibility studies, structuring and transactions advisory

Investment Promotion

Proactive investor engagement and market intelligence. The DRC Investment Summit will host focused investment opportunity sessions through boardroom discussions

Policy Environment

Advocating institutional reforms and improving regulatory policies to promote policy certainty for the private sector stakeholders. Creating a business friendly environment
